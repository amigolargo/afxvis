In early 2015, Aphex Twin began uploading rare and unreleased music to an anonymous Soundcloud account. By late October he had uploaded 261 tracks, and a fan voting site had appeared to help Aphex Twin himself decide which tracks should get remastered and released traditionally. This visualisation plots fan votes against Soundcloud plays to create a new visual means to explore the archive.
